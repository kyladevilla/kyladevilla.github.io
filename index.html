Kyla de Villa

I am a PhD Candidate at UC Berkeley in the Department of Earth and Planetary Science. I use computer simulations (ab initio and machine learning methods) to study the properties of materials under the extreme conditions relevant to planetary interiors. My current project involves studying transport properties of planetary ices, with implications for the structure of ice-giant interiors and magnetic field generation for these planets.
